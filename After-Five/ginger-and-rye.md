Ginger and Rye 
==============

Ingredients 
------------
* 1 part George Dickel rye whiskey
* 2 parts ginger ale 
* 1 dash of chuncho, krangostura, or lavender bitters

Procedure 
---------- 
Don't shake it; just swirl it.  

Notes
------ 
These are the bitters that don't work well
* blood orange biters
* grapefruit bitters 
* teapot bitters
