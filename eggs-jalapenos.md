Eggs Jalapenos
===========

Ingredients
------------

 + eggs
 + bacon
 + red jalapenos
 + white onion
 + cheddar

Procedure 
---------

1. Fry the bacon
2. Cook chopped onions and jalapenos in bacon grease 
3. Add eggs
4. Add cheese salt and pepper right before you remove the eggs from heat 


Notes
------
 * red jalapenos not green
